# Recommendation Memo

## Executive Summary

The experiment compared the existing onboarding process (Control) with a new onboarding campaign (Treatment).

The Treatment group achieved significantly higher conversion rates and engagement metrics.

## North Star Metric

Paid Conversion Rate

## Experiment Results

Control Conversion Rate: 3.17%

Treatment Conversion Rate: 6.99%

P-value: 0.00115

## Guardrail Analysis

* Engagement Score improved from 57.03 to 62.93.
* Average Days to Convert decreased from 8.86 to 6.40.
* Revenue per User increased from 51.75 to 53.88.

No significant negative impact was observed.

## Final Recommendation

Launch the Treatment onboarding experience to all users.

## Risks

* Long-term retention was not measured.
* Future monitoring is recommended.

## Next Steps

* Roll out treatment.
* Monitor conversion and retention.
* Track refund and support ticket rates.
