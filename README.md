# AyushiBokare_bitsom_ba_2511050_part2_kpi_experiment
# Business Context

A subscription-based digital product company launched a new onboarding campaign to improve user conversion and engagement.

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains user-level experiment data for Control and Treatment groups, including conversion, engagement, revenue, and onboarding metrics.

## North Star Metric

Paid Conversion Rate

Reason:
Paid Conversion Rate directly measures business growth and subscription success.

## KPI Tree Summary

The North Star Metric is driven by:

* Acquisition
* Activation
* Engagement
* Revenue

Guardrail Metrics:

* Refund Rate
* Support Ticket Rate
* Days To Convert

## Experiment Analysis Approach

The analysis compared Control and Treatment groups using conversion, engagement, and revenue metrics.

## Hypothesis Test Summary

Control Conversion Rate: 3.17%

Treatment Conversion Rate: 6.99%

P-value: 0.00115

Result:
Statistically significant improvement.

## Guardrail Metrics Considered

* Engagement Score
* Days To Convert
* Revenue Per User

## Final Recommendation

Launch the Treatment onboarding experience to all users.

## Assumptions

* Experiment groups are representative.
* Revenue data is valid.

## Limitations

* Long-term retention was not measured.
* Further monitoring is recommended.
